Smart Urban Mobility System • FlowOptimal

What is wrong

Urban traffic congestion, particularly at signalized intersections, causes massive economic losses, environmental damage, and reduced quality of life. Current traffic management systems operate on fixed timers or simple vehicle detection, failing to adapt to real-time traffic fluctuations and lacking predictive capabilities.

Our Solution

We propose an integrated system that combines IoT sensing with AI-driven optimization. Four ultrasonic sensors at each intersection monitor traffic queues in real-time. This data feeds into two complementary machine learning models: a Reinforcement Learning system that dynamically adjusts traffic signals, and a Predictive Analytics model that forecasts traffic patterns for route recommendations. Results are delivered through a userfriendly web application that advises drivers on optimal travel times and routes.
